    Exploring Tokyo, Japan

    📅 Dates: May 15 - May 25, 2024 📍 Location: Tokyo, Japan 🏨 Accommodation: Shibuya Hotel

    An incredible journey through the vibrant streets of Tokyo. From the bustling Shibuya Crossing to the serene Meiji Shrine, every moment was a blend of modern technology and traditional culture. Highlights included trying authentic ramen, experiencing the unique gaming arcades, and witnessing the breathtaking cherry blossoms.

    Safari Adventure in Kenya

    📅 Dates: July 5 - July 15, 2024 📍 Location: Masai Mara, Kenya 🏕️ Accommodation: Luxury Tented Camp

    An unforgettable safari experience in the heart of the Masai Mara National Reserve. Witnessed the incredible wildlife - lions, elephants, giraffes, and the famous wildebeest migration. The nights under the starry African sky, listening to the sounds of the wilderness, were truly magical.
